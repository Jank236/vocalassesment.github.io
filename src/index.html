This is a tool meant for describing voices via sliders. You can share via screenshots. Hover over any title to get a description (otherwise, like for mobile, scroll down for descriptions).

Blue color is associated with masculine, pink is associated with feminine. Green is generally considered subjectively (or in the case of FVF, objectively) good. Red is usually considered undesirable. Just because a voice has masculine qualities doesn't mean it isn't feminine and vice versa. Same with undesirable qualities; you can have a great voice despite leaning into those territories.

Descriptions:

Size:

How sound is affected by the shape and amount of space in the throat.

Weight:

Thickness and thinness of the folds.

Fullness:

The proportion that the size and weight are balanced.

- Full

The vocal weight and vocal size are perceived to be balanced.

- Underfullness

The voice is perceived to be disportionately light weight to given size.

- Overfullness

The voice is perceived to be disportionately heavy weight to given size.

Octave:

A space of eight steps between musical notes. How high or low a pitch is.

Abduction:

How open or closed the vocal folds are. If they are open they are abducted, if they are closed it is adducted.

Volumetric Air Pressure:

The amount of air in volume.

False Vocal Folds:

Amount of constriction to close to protect the true folds.

Nasality:

Air going through the nasal passage when speaking.

Gendered Inflection:

Speech patterns related to a gender.
